Our self-pour technology consists of software, touchscreens and electronic components that support a seamless customer experience.
Touchscreen
The touchscreen, which displays information about the brewer, the alcohol percentage and the price per ounce, is typically installed above the tap
Radio frecuency card
With the iSuds system, after ID verification, the customers receive an RFID card to control the beer tap
Pour it
Once they have poured a total of 32 ounces, the taps will no longer serve them. They must check with an employee to reactivate their card
Payment
When they check out, their credit card is automatically charged
Cheers
It’s a simple streamlined experience for both the customer and the business
