Self-Serve Beer Technology
Simple & Fun!
Friendly
iSuds is state of the art technology in a box. Visual display on the tablet shows much more than the type of beer. Restaurants can advertise on each screen. And specials, news and events can be showcased.
Statistics
The system tracks all data, so the owner knows his average pour size, average charge per ounce and average ticket size. He can also see what beers are selling the best on a daily basis, how the different products are trending over time, and track customer spending.
Variety
iSuds has emerged as a tool to allow beer (and wine) lovers to indulge in the vast selection of offerings. Not only does the self-pour beer wall allow the customer to sample beer at will, but it also enables the restaurant to maximize revenue by offering more variety, including more premium-priced offerings, and it significantly reduces "giveaways" and product waste.
Profit
According to industry sources, self-pour beer walls generate as much as 40 percent more revenue than traditional bartenders. That’s a lot of profits!
